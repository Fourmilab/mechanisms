
This directory contains unmodified copies of the CMU motion capture
database converted to DAZ forman and published at:

    https://www.sites.google.com/a/cgspeed.com/cgspeed/motion-capture/daz-friendly-release

They are included purely to permit this directory tree to be 
self-contained without the need to include the entire 5.2 gigabyte 
archive, most of which we don't use.
